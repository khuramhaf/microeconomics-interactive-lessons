Understanding Price Elasticity of Demand

This interactive lesson explores how the Price Elasticity of Demand (PED) changes as you move between two points on the demand curve. We use the percentage formula, where Point A represents the old (initial) point and Point B represents the new point. Move the points and observe how changes in price, quantity demanded, and elasticity update in real time.


How to Use the Playground

  • Drag Point A and Point B along the demand curve.
  • Watch the Price Elasticity of Demand change in real time as you move the points.
  • Explore how elasticity varies at different locations on the demand curve.
